Sweet Potato Fries Recipe

Ingredients
2 large sweet potatoes
Garlic Powder
Salt, Pepper, Paprika
1 tbs Oil Olive
Thyme
Oregano
  1. PreHeat oven to 450 degrees
  2. Cut potatoes to 1/4 inch thin strips
  3. Mix all other ingredients in a large bowl
  4. Miz potatoes in large bowl until they are well coated
  5. 1 1/2 tsp Paprika
  6. Cover baking sheet in parchment paper
  7. Transfer Potatoes to baking sheet, spacing htem so they dont not overlap
  8. place in oven for 25-30 min, turning over every 10 mins.
  9. remove from oven and enjoy!
